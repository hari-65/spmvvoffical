    TALENTS DAY REPORT

    Talents day and Engineers day are celebrated at School of Engineering and Technology, SPMVV, Tirupati,
    on 15th September 2018 Chief guests Prof. V. Uma - Rector of the University, Prof. A. Ramakrishna Rao- Director of SoET, N.T.V. Ram Kumar-DSP, District special branch, Chittoor
    and Smt. V.Sulochana- Assistant General Manager of Amara Raja Batteries and Prof. J. Kathyayani, Convener of Student Innovative Society, graced the occasion with their motivational words.
    The main motto of the talents day is to bring out the innate talents of the students.
    In this regard various competitions like Dance, Singing, Jam and Painting have been conducted to the students and prizes were distributed to the winners.
    Students took active participation in cultural programmers with unique themes and made the audience spellbound.
    The programmer concluded with a vote of thanks by cultural coordinator Dr. A. Haritha
