OUR FACULTY

Dr. A. Ramakrishna Rao

Director

Dr. A. Ramakrishna Rao is a Professor and Director of School of Engineering and Technology, Sri Padmavati Mahila Vivavidyalayam, Tirupati since 2016. He received his B. Tech in Mechanical Engineering from Sri Venkateswara University, Tirupati in 1975, M.Tech in Industrial Engineering from Sri Venkateswara University, Tirupati in 1978 and he received his Ph.D. from IIT kharagpur in 1989. Dr.Ramakrishna Rao has several years of work experience in teaching, research and academic administration roles. He provided his services in various roles as Professor, Head of the Department, Principal and Dean in SV University college of Engineering during the period 1979-2013. He guided more than 70 M.Tech projects and under his Supervision 8 Ph.Ds. are awarded. He strongly beliefs in the principle that “Hardworking and sincerity will always bring success”.
